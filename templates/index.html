How it works:

  1. Click the "Start Continuous Recording" button
  2. Speak naturally - your voice will be analyzed continuously
  3. The system will update the detected tone every few seconds
  4. Your emotional tone will be displayed as an emoji with description
  5. Your actual speech will be transcribed and displayed (in supported browsers)
  6. Click the button again to stop recording and see your overall tone summary
