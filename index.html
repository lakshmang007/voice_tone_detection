üîä How It Works

üòä

The application analyzes your voice in real-time

  1. Click the "Start Continuous Recording" button
  2. Speak naturally - your voice will be analyzed continuously
  3. The system detects when you're actually speaking
  4. Your emotional tone is displayed as an emoji with description
  5. Click the button again to stop recording

Try speaking in different tones to see how the detected emotion changes!
